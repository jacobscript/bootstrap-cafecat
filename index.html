The Cats Rule!

In order to provide the best experience for people and cats, we ask you observe the following rules:

Visiting CafeCat

Feel free to take photos

Sanitize your hands before entering the play area

Do not bring any of your own animals

Children under 13 must be accompanied by an adult

Interacting with the Cats

Do not pull on cat’s tail

Do not disturb cats if they are eating

Do not give human food to cats

Toys for the cats are available throughout the play area

Special Rules for Visitors

Parents: Please supervise your children

Absolutely no running

Absolutely no yelling

If a cat is hiding, let it hide

If a cat is sleeping, let it sleep
